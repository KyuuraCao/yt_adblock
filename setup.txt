- got to setting -> filter list -> checklist the auto update filters list and my filters -> apply now -> update now
- Go to My filters and copy this code 
   
  youtube.com##+js(set, yt.config_.openPopupConfig.supportedPopups.adBlockMessageViewModel, false)

  youtube.com##+js(set, Object.prototype.adBlocksFound, 0)

  youtube.com##+js(set, ytplayer.config.args.raw_player_response.adPlacements, [])

  youtube.com##+js(set, Object.prototype.hasAllowedInstreamAd, true)

  Apply changes

- Restar your ublock Origin an Enjoy your ad free youtube experience :D
